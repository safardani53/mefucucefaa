Chris Mann: Engaged to Laura Perloe!

 Posted on March 8, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  149 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Chris Mann of The Voice is engaged! To Laura Perloe!

The singing show’s finalist accompanied her to the L.A. premiere of Seeking a Friend for the End of the World on Monday – Perloe plays a small role – and broke the news.

Mann said they got engaged four weeks ago. Nice!

“He had just gotten of The Voice, so it had been craziness,” Perloe said.

“We decided we wanted to take a few days to ourselves and go up to Napa and just kind of relax … Reconnect with each other. We went to one of our favorite Vineyards and he did it the old fashioned way… got down on one knee!”

“It was nice,” Mann, who ultimately lost out to Jermaine Paul, but won in life, agreed. “It was our first time alone in six months because it was chaotic. Now it’s fun.”

Congratulations!
