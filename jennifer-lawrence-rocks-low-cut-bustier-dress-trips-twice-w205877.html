Jennifer Lawrence Rocks Low-Cut Bustier Dress, Trips Twice

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  253 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Sizzling in the U.K.! Jennifer Lawrence heated up the X-Men: Apocalypse afterparty in London, England, on Monday, May 9, wearing a sultry lace bustier dress.

The knee-length frock — her second design of the night — featured a black lingerie-inspired bodice and a multicolored floral patterned skirt. Lawrence, 25, paired the sexy ensemble with lace-up Jimmy Choo heels.

As she walked out of Tape nightclub, where she partied with costars including Game of Thrones‘ Sophie Turner, she lost her footing and stumbled.

The Hunger Games alum is prone to tripping in heels and famously ended up on hands and knees en route to accepting her first Oscar. And she had another near-fall while trying to take cover from the rain on the Apocalypse red carpet, just hours earlier at BFI IMAX. Lawrence quickly regained her balance in her backless Dior ensemble, paired with metallic Jimmy Choo heels, diamond rings and bracelets.

The Oscar winner, who plays blue-skinned Mystique in the action flick, admitted to Extra that taking off her makeup after filming was a huge turn-off for ex-boyfriend Nicholas Hoult, who also stars in the X-Men series. “[Men’s shaving cream is] really good for taking [body] paint off, but Nick used to hate it ’cause I’d come home smelling like a man and too tired,” she revealed. “I’d be like, ‘Don’t touch me, I’m too tired,’ and he’d be like, ‘Well, you smell like a dude.'”
