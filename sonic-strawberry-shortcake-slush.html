Sonic launches sweet new menu item - but there's a catch

 Posted on October 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  405 words  |  Larita Shotwell

SONIC Drive-In is launching a brand new menu item tomorrow that’s perfect for cooling down on summer days.

The fast food chain has a new beverage headed to its 3,500 restaurants.

Sonic is introducing a Strawberry Shortcake Snowball Slush Float on May 29.

Priced at $2.99 for a medium, the slush features several layers of flavor to accompany the store’s popular burgers, hot dogs, and fries.

The beverage is made with real strawberries, creamy ice cream, and signature Sonic slush.

The slush is topped with vanilla ice cream and sugar crystals to mimic the crunchy texture of a real-life snowball.

Mackenzie Gibson, vice president of culinary and menu innovation at Sonic, reckons customers will love how the new item “provides a delicious respite from the summer heat.”

“This Snowball Slush Float is the perfect summertime treat to sip on while road-tripping, heading to the beach, or barbecuing with family,” she said in a press release.

THE CATCH

However, the beverage will only be on the menu for a limited time.

Sonic confirmed to The U.S. Sun that it will be available through July while supplies last.

LIFE’S A BEACH

Sonic has also announced the arrival of several new merchandise items in time for the summer.

Beach and pool products with fun, Sonic branding will be available to buy online at sonicswagshop.com, from June 1.

The items include:

  • a pool float in the shape of a cherry limeade drink
  • a cabana set featuring a Sonic pattern
  • retro cherry red sunglasses
  • a sonic beach towel, cooler bag, and summer tumbler

MORE FAST FOOD NEWS

But Sonic isn’t the only fast food restaurant that is introducing new items to excite customers.

Available from May 15 Burger King is offering a new Whopper burger in partnership with Spider-Man.

The Spider-Verse Whopper features a red bun with black sesame seeds, in tribute to the Marvel character’s distinctive costume.

The offer comes as part of National Hamburger Month celebrations.

On top of this, Wendy’s has a whole host of other deals available until the end of May, including a buy one, get one for $1 deal on burgers.
