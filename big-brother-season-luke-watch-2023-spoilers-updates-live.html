Big Brother Season 25 LIVE Fans slam show after 'unfair' Power of Veto challenge and Matt left i

 Posted on April 9, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  917 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

BIG Brother houseguest Matt Klotz was seen in tears on the live feeds following this week's "unfair" veto competition, fans say.

Matt is the first Deaf houseguest to compete on the show, and the most recent competition for the golden Power of Veto required competitors to listen to clues to solve riddles - leaving fans outraged for Matt's sake.

Fans took to X, formerly known as Twitter, to slam the move as one wrote: "I cannot believe they did this comp with a deaf player, that's completely unfair."

Others noted that Matt appeared to be crying on the house's 24/7 live feeds as one viewer shared, "How exactly was Matt supposed to do this Omg ??? No wonder he was crying. I feel so bad for him."

"I am sure the comps are designed well before the cast is finalized BUT they should have either scrapped this one or adapted those clues to non-audible ones. This is disgusting," another slammed.

Meanwhile, Reilly Smedley was evicted from the Big Brother house by a vote of 12-0 Thursday night.
