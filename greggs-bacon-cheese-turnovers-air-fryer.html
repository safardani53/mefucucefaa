I made Greggs style bacon and cheese turnovers in my air fryer - they are so good and take minutes

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  275 words  |  Aldo Pusey

THEY are one of Greggs most popular items – the delicious bacon and cheese turnover.

But did you know you can make your own from home in just minutes? 

TikTok user @lovefoodeatmore, which is run by a foodie couple called Rach and Josh, shared how they make their own version in their air fryer.

The pair, who are from Kent, said that all it takes is five simple steps, starting with cutting some pre-made pastry into medium sized squares.

Next, they placed a rasher of bacon on top of the pastry, lying it out diagonally. 

Lastly, they added a generous amount of cheese on top of the bacon – 300g to be precise – but you can choose to have as much or as little as you like. 

Read More in Food

Then, all you have to do is roll the turnover up in a burrito style, glaze them with an egg and pop it into the air fryer for 10-12 minutes at 180 degrees. 

Once the timer goes off, you’ll have delicious puffy pastry and melted cheese – and the results are amazing. 

The couple wrote: “These honestly take 10 minutes to make and are as good as the ones you’d get from Greggs.

“Give them a try and tell us what you think.”
