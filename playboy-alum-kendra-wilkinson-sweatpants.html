FORMER Playboy model Kendra Wilkinson appeared to start off 2020 in a slump - just days after announcing she "feels like s**t."

The star, donning sweats, didn't seem to be her usual bubbly, light-hearted self, an onlooker told The Sun.

An eyewitness told The Sun of the January 2 outing in L.A.: “She was not her usual bubbly self, bouncing around laughing.”

The glum sighting came days after she posted cryptic, moody tweets.

The 34-year-old mom tweeted on December 27: "I feel like s**t, my gut feels like s**t so I’m going on a mini cleanse."

In another tweet she shared: "Sometimes I just get this uneasy gut feeling that something is off.

"Maybe just need to s**t. Lol"

She’s still on the outs with mom Patti Wilkinson, 60, and spent the holidays instead with her best friend, Jessica Hall, and her family.

In October Kendra and her wealthy businessman boyfriend Donald “DJ” Friese broke up after being together for just four months.

Radar Online reported the two attended Paris Hilton’s Halloween party on October 25, and then had a huge fight the next day.

A source said: “Kendra got really mad at him and dumped him."

Earlier in 2019, her divorce to former NFL player Hank Baskett, 37, was finalized after nine years of marriage.

According to their divorce decree, they split joint legal and physical custody of their children — son Hank IV, 9, and daughter Alijah Mary, 4.

A source close to Kendra told People: “It’s still surreal. She’s been waiting to put this all behind her."

Kendra's five-year reality show Kendra on Top has also still not been renewed for another season.

But things may be finally looking up for Kendra, who found fame as one of the late Hugh Hefner's blond bombshell girlfriends on E! reality show The Girls Next Door.

She tweeted earlier today: "Exciting day. Will be announcing something soon. 2020 so far has been the best year ever..."

She also just announced a new collaboration with ShoeDazzle.
