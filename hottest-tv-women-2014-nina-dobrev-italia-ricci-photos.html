Hottest TV Women

Note: these are simply our opinions and did not want to leave out anyone — I totally have about 100 women I’d love to include.

Here’s who we narrowed it down to and why (in no order):

Nina Dobrev: The Vampire Diaries
Not only did Nina play about 385720 characters during TVD, but her confidence and natural beauty is so stunning. Even when she’s hanging with friends wearing no makeup, she looks amazing.

Kerry Washington: Scandal
I mean, this doesn’t really need a description but Kerry Washington is 37 years old and can still look so gorgeous. Whether she’s in a sex scene or carrying a baby, Olivia Pope is flawless.

Lea Michele: Glee
It’s the final season of Glee, and we’re definitely not ready to say goodbye to Lea anytime soon. Sure she may have been a waitress on her Sons of Anarchy guest appearance, but we still think she’s even more glorious than Rachel Berry.

Andi Dorfman: The Bachelorette
The gowns, the girls, and well… the roses. Andi was definitely one of the best Bachelorettes to ever compete on the show, and she totally landed a HOTTIE (Yes, we’re looking at you Josh.)

Shay Mitchell: Pretty Little Liars
Shay takes on the role of a lesbian who gets into quite a bit of trouble on PLL, and we can’t help but add on that she does a hell of a good job — and looks amazing doing it.

Italia Ricci: Chasing Life
Italia may play one of the most positive characters in TV. April is going through cancer and all of its horrible processes, but her positivity (and her wigs) keep her smiling throughout!

Gwen Stefani: The Voice
It was Gwen’s first stint as a judge on The Voice, and she may not have won but she got our votes! She looked stunning week after week and at 45, we’re convinced she’s aging backward.

Emilia Clarke: Game of Thrones
Last but absolutely not least, we have Emilia, who has conquered dragons, men, and a lot more. Needless to say, we’ve seen quite a bit of her on the show, and she is definitely the whole package

Alright guys, we need to know what you think! Who do you think is the hottest TV star of 2014? Let us know!
