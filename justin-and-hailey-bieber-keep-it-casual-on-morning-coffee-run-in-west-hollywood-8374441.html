Justin and Hailey Bieber Are Low-Key on Morning Coffee Run

 Posted on March 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  577 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Justin Bieber and Hailey Baldwin Bieber were looking low-key as they made a pit-stop for coffee over the weekend. 

The couple were spotted in West Hollywood on Sunday as they grabbed coffee and breakfast together.

Hailey, 26, was dressed down compared to her previous appearance at Kim Kardashian’s 43rd birthday bash on Friday night. On Sunday, she wore a pair of low-rise, wide leg navy trousers with a belt and a cropped vintage Calvin Klein jeans top. She paired the outfit with a black shoulder bag, her signature bedazzled “B” necklace, red snakeskin boots and a pair of black sunglasses. 

Justin, 29, also kept things casual as he wore a black button-down with brown trousers and a pair of fluffy blue slippers. Like his wife of five years, he wore a pair of black sunglasses, as well as a backwards black-and-white polka dot hat. The “Ghost” singer was also blinged out as he rocked a gold watch, several rings and two bedazzled mushroom chains around his neck for the morning outing. 

The Biebers' outing came after they’d spent some precious quality time with friends’ babies in recent days, including 11-month-old Lava Louise, daughter of their close friends Joe Termini and Kelia Moniz Termini.

On Friday, Hailey shared a short clip on her Instagram Story showing her and her husband showering their friends’ daughter with love as Hailey held her and Justin planted several sweet kisses on her head, with his wife looking on adoringly.

She also shared a sweet shot of what appeared to be Lava Louise sitting on Justin’s lap, as well as an adorable photo of the baby kissing herself in the mirror with the caption “mood.”

Days before that, the couple had met another one of their friends’ newborns, former E! News host Jason Kennedy and Lauren Kennedy’s daughter, Poppy Ford, whom Justin affectionately referred to as his “niece” in an Instagram post last week.

“Anyone who knows @thejasonkennedy and @thelaurenkennedy know their journey and challenges with having kids,” Justin wrote as he shared two photos of him holding the infant and feeding her a bottle. “They now have two beautiful babies that I’m absolutely obsessed with. 😩😩😩 ❤️‍🔥❤️‍🔥❤️‍🔥.”

“I love you so much 🥹🫂,” Jason, 41, commented on the sweet post.

He and his wife, Lauren, 35, welcomed Poppy, their second child, just days before Justin met her, on Oct. 10, calling her their “angel” in a joint post on Instagram. 

As for whether kids are on the horizon for the Bieber’s themselves, Hailey opened up to GQ Hype about the fascination that revolves around whether or not she’s pregnant at a given moment in time, calling it “so hilarious how much people f---ing care.”

She said being a mother “is something that I look forward to,” adding, “It’s also such a private, intimate thing. It’s something that's going to come when it comes.”
