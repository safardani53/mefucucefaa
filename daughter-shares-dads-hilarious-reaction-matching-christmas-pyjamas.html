SCROLLING through Instagram, you’ll find many families wearing matching pyjamas today. 

Most think it’s an adorable addition to the classic family photo. 

But daughters have taken to TikTok to share their dad’s honest reactions to the idea - and people are finding it hilarious. 

Megan, known online as @megangom3, shared her dad’s reaction to the family wearing matching pyjamas.

Their sets were made up of a grey long-sleeve top decorated with a teddy bear, and matching bottoms. 

During the 40-second video, her dad commented: “Please…I don’t want to be like this.

Fans loved the honest reaction, with the video gaining more than 3,700 likes and 84,000 views. 

In the comments, Megan’s fans completed the outfit, with one writing: “He looks lovely in them pj's.”

Another said: “Aw you all look great. Don’t worry most of the UK are all matching tonight.”

Kate, known online as @kateabbieandleo, also filmed her dad’s honest feelings towards his festive loungewear set. 

She wrote: “When you buy your dad matching pjs for Christmas and want group photos with your one-year-old.”

During the 35-second video, her dad is seen sighing, grimacing and shaking his head as his family get into position for the photo around him. 

Her video also attracted plenty of attention, gaining more than 92,000 likes and 1.6 million views in just 24 hours.
