DO you wish your family mucked in with the housework more? Well, you can now buy a Henry vacuum cleaner for your child – and it actually works.

The £16.99 toy is a replica of the much-loved multi-purpose hoover, which retails for £124.99.

According to the product’s description, it picks up “small bits of paper waste or small poly beads”.

The waste is then “sucked up into a removable drawer, which is quick and simple to empty”.

The cord can be reeled in by turning a handle, just like the real thing, and it also comes with an accessories pack.
