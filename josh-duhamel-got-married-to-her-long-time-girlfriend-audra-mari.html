Josh Duhamel Got Married To Her Long-Time Girlfriend Audra Mari

 Posted on October 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  507 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Josh Duhamel, an American actor, married his longtime girlfriend Audra Mari on September 10, 2022. According to Us Weekly, the 49-year-old actor and Mari exchanged vows in North Dakota’s Olivet Lutheran Church in Fargo. Page Six acquired photos of Audra wearing a white dress with a high neckline and a veil over her hair bun. Josh Duhamel, on the other hand, looked dashing in a three-piece suit.

According to the magazine, an eyewitness saw the couple “posing and gleefully pumping their fists in the air” after their wedding. Josh Duhamel and Audra Mari announced their engagement in January 2022, and nine months later they married.

Josh Duhamel and Audra Mari were first seen in public in 2019.

Duhamel and Audra Mari, who have a 21-year age gap, were first seen together in May 2019. According to Entertainment Tonight, a source said that the couple initially met after being connected by mutual acquaintances.

“Josh and Audra met via common acquaintances and get along incredibly well; they have a fantastic time together and agree on what they want their future to be.”

They were first seen together publicly on a date night in Malibu in May 2019. Their romance was revealed in October of the same year when they were caught kissing at Toronto International Airport. The Las Vegas star discussed his future and desire for more children with Dax Shepard on the Armchair Expert podcast:

“I’m not 30 anymore,” Duhamel said. “I want to have more kids in the next few years, so it’s more about finding someone young enough to have kids. It’s not like I’m out there just f***ing everything; that’s really not who I am. I’m looking for a female with whom I can be and have a family.”

After almost four years together, the Safe Haven actor proposed, and the couple married in January 2022. Josh Duhamel shared a joyous photo of the pair on Instagram to announce the exciting news. He captioned the photo as follows:

“It’s on!! She discovered a note in a bottle washed up on the beach and said YES!!”

Duhamel shared his spontaneous proposal plan for Audra Mari weeks after popping the big question. He stated:

“I simply decided at the last minute that was how I wanted to do it.” I hadn’t planned anything. I didn’t yet have a ring. ‘OK, you’re going to do this today,’ I said. Today is the day.’ And that’s exactly what occurred. … I worked with [the hotel staff] to ensure the bottle was floating up to the beach as we walked out there.”

Josh Duhamel is not getting married for the first time. The Transformers star was previously married to singer Fergie. From 2009 until 2019, the couple was married and had a nine-year-old son, Axl Jack Duhamel.
