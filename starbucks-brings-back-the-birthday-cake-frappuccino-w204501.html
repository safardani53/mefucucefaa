Starbucks Brings Back the Birthday Cake Frappuccino

 Posted on September 15, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  168 words  |  Larita Shotwell

We’ve got good news and bad news for Starbucks lovers (yes, you, Harry Styles!).

To mark its 21st anniversary, the coffee company is reintroducing one of its most beloved Frappuccino flavors, but it’s here for only a limited time.

Back by popular demand, the Birthday Cake Frappuccino has once again found its place on Starbucks’ chalkboard menus. The sweet and festive drink is a blend of vanilla bean and hazelnut, topped with rosy-hued raspberry-flavored whipped cream.

The frozen beverage was a hit back in March 2015, when baristas first started whipping it up for customers in honor of Starbucks’ 20th birthday. To celebrate its short-term encore engagement, the java giant has even created a Birthday Cake Frappuccino emoji for the Starbucks Keyboard — which you can send to your Frapp-loving friends, starting April 28.

The Birthday Cake Frappuccino will be sold in participating Starbucks locations in the U.S. and Canada for just five days, starting Thursday, April 28, until Monday, May 2.
