BEYONCE looks bootylicious in a pinstripe blazer and thigh-high footwear as she enjoys a date night in London with husband Jay-Z.

The superstar was snapped at swanky private members club Oswald’s in Mayfair.

Bey, 41, is currently on the UK leg of her Renaissance world tour and starts a run of five concerts at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium on Monday.

The mum-of-three and rapper Jay-Z, 53, celebrated their 15th wedding anniversary last month.

Seems she’s still glad he put a ring on it.

We recently revealed how she spent nearly £2,000 on chicken meals from Nando’s while on the UK leg of her Renaissance tour.

But it is not as if she would struggle to pay for the £1,800 treat for her crew — the global tour is expected to gross £2billion in ticket sales.

Beyoncé, called in the mother of all orders at Nando’s in Edinburgh before her gig at Murrayfield on Saturday night.

The Single Ladies (Put A Ring On It) singer sent the kitchen at Fountain Park into a frenzy by requesting 110 chicken burgers and 90 pittas.
