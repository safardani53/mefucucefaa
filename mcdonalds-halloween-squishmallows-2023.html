McDonald's Halloween Squishmallows: Can I get the limited-edition plushies?

 Posted on February 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  312 words  |  Aldo Pusey

MCDONALD'S has become famous for its Happy Meal that often come with a special toy for children.

Among the most popular are the soft plushies, with this year's Halloween Squishmallows already in high demand - but are they available in the US?

What are McDonald's Halloween Squishmallows?

The McDonald's Halloween Squishmallows are limited-edition plushies.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Squishmallows became extremely popular as many began sharing their collection via social media.

The plushie brand debuted in 2017 when Kelly Toys Holdings LLC released the first eight original Squishmallows.

Since then the brand has collaborated with an array of brands including Pokémon and Disney with McDonald's being the latest.

When will the Squishmallows become available?

The McDonald's Halloween Squishmallows are available in the United Kingdom and Ireland only, which began on October 11, 2023.

In the United States, kids will have to wait until later this year to get their hands on the plushies with their Happy Meal, with a date yet to be confirmed.

Currently, American customers are able to collect Pokemon toys with their food.

People have been taking to TikTok to share their findings and users on the app are sharing their excitement to get their own.

What kinds of Squishmallows are available?

There are six different Squishmallows available at McDonald's.

These options include:

  • Bella the spider
  • Patricio the bat
  • Brock the Batdog
  • Stump the skeleton cat
  • Wade the werewolf
  • Autumn the cat

According to TikTok users, the Squishmallow logo is embroidered.
