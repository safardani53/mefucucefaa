DEALS and discounts come and go, but 60% off is hard to come by.

For Amazon shoppers, one TikTok creator has found a way to bring these sales to the top of your search.

You'll never miss a great deal again.

And with each year, the website becomes harder to navigate.

How do you get 60% off?

First, log in to Amazon and start a product search.

Next, copy this code: &pct-off=60-

Then, paste the code on the end of the url.

You'll want to keep the rest of the url the way it is.

Just tack "&pct-off=60-" on the end.

After you hit enter, your search will list every item that is 60% off at the top.
