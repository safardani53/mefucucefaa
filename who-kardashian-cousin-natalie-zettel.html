Who is Kardashians' cousin Natalie Zettel?

THE KARDASHIAN family is arguably one of the most famous families in the world.

While most fans are familiar with the Kardashian-Jenner family tree, some are unaware of their relationship with Natalie Zettel and now they want to know all about her.

Who is Kardashians' cousin Natalie Zettel?

The Kardashian/Jenner clan is made up of mom, Kris Jenner, Caitlyn Jenner, sisters Kourtney, Kim, Khloe, Kendall, Kylie, and brother Rob, who was named after the late Robert Kardashian.

Outside of the immediate family members is Kris' sister, Karen Houghton, who is the mother of Natalie Zettel, aka one of the Kardashians' "secret" cousins.

Natalie first made headlines in 2019 after she appeared in a random photo alongside Kylie, which quickly caused fans to wonder about her connection to the famous family.

After it was determined that she was related, fans were able to find other events over the years that she attended, including Kim’s KKW Beauty event in December 2018, according to Cosmopolitan.

At this time, little information is available on Natalie's career because she often keeps details of her personal life under wraps. Still, she does post about her adventures on Instagram to over 55,000 followers.

Who is Natalie Zettel's mom?

Natalie was born in the late 1990s to Karen and her ex-husband Mark Zettel.

While little information is available on Natalie's dad, Karen is famously known as the sister of momager Kris.

The pair grew up in San Diego and were raised by their single mother, Mary Jo Campbell, following her divorce from their father, Robert Houghton.

Outside of being known as Kris' sister, she is known for her career as a part-time nurse and cookbook author.

She released her first cookbook, Naturally Gourmet Cookbook, in 2010 and released Vol 2 of the book in 2013.

In Kris's 2011 autobiography, she said about her sister: "We loved each other, and we were there for each other through thick and thin, and to this day, we are a part of each other's lives."

What is Natalie Zettel's net worth?

Along with being one of the most famous families in the world, the Kardashians are also one of the richest families in the world with a combined net worth of over $2billion.

As for Natalie, her net worth is not as high and is only estimated to be $1million.

At this time, the main source of her income is unclear.
