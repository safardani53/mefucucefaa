Who is Jamie Redknapps wife Frida Andersson-Lourie?

 Posted on March 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  348 words  |  Martina Birk

JAMIE Redknapp found a new lease of life when he met Frida Andersson-Lourie in 2019.

Since then the couple have been very busy, and are now married and have even welcomed a baby together.

Who is Frida Andersson-Lourie?

Frida Andersson-Lourie is a model who was born on October 31, 1983.

She hails from Sweden, but has lived in London since her modelling career took off.

Frida is signed to Mrs Robinson Management and has modelled for brands such as Escada.

Like her husband Jamie, she is a divorcee and was previously married to American hedge fund manager Jonathan Lourie.

When did Frida Andersson-Lourie and Jamie Redknapp get married?

Jamie and Frida tied the knot on October 18, 2021, at the Chelsea Registry Office in London.

They got married just weeks before their baby was born - four years on from Jamie's divorce from Louise Redknapp. 

The newlyweds were joined by 30 close family and friends for the discreet ceremony.

The couple, who met via mutual pals in 2019, have remained out of the limelight since they started dating - going public with their romance in August 2020.

An Insider told Heat at the time: “Jamie feels ready to move on from his marriage to Louise and is happy to be doing it with someone who also has kids and is financially secure."

The source added: "Anyone who knows Jamie knows that his sons are his main priority and mean everything to him, so he likes that Frida’s a parent.

"She’s also a homebody and into fitness, like he is.”

Meanwhile, Jamie was married to singer Louise Redknapp from 1998 until 2017, when they announced they had split.

How many children does Frida Andersson-Lourie have?

Frida has four children, who she shares with her ex-husband, Jonathan Lourie.

With the birth of Raphael on November 24, 2021, she has become a mother of five.

Meanwhile, Jamie himself has two sons of his own.

He has sons Charley (born July 27, 2004) and Beau (November 10, 2008) from his previous relationship with Louise.
