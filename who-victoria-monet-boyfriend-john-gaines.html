Who is Victoria Monét's boyfriend, John Gaines?

Outside of being known as Monét's boyfriend, Gaines is known for his career as a fitness model and trainer.

He runs the fitness company Gains By Gaines Fitness, which offers personalized programs that help clients "burn fat, build muscle and feel the best you've ever felt!"

A subscription for Gaines' program starts at $39.99 per month and includes a personalized workout program and meal plan as well as a shopping list and fitness videos, among other things.

Gaines discovered his passion for fitness at an early age due to his career as a multi-sport athlete and after the competition stopped, he focused on the next chapter of his life as a trainer.
