TODAY'S Sheinelle Jones has shared photos with her husband, Uche Oieh, and their kids in a rare TV moment.

The daytime TV host recapped her family weekend during Monday's show by sharing snaps from her twins' birthday party.

It happened during the third hour of Today, where Sheinelle, 45, and her co-hosts, Al Roker, 68, and Craig Melvin, 44, noted highlights from their weekends.

Craig went first by detailing his trip to the 2023 Belmont Stakes horse race with his wife, Lindsay Czarniak, 45, and their two kids: Delano, eight, and Sybil, six.

Al then summarized his jam-packed days off, where he went to dinner at downtown New York City restaurant, Raf's, with his wife, Deborah Roberts, 62, before cheering on his Today family participating in a 10K run in the park.

Lastly, Sheinelle recalled spending her weekend at American Dream Mall in East Rutherford, New Jersey, to celebrate her kids, Uche and Clara's ' 11th birthday.
