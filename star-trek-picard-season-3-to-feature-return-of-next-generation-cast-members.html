Picard Season 3 To Feature Return Of Next Generation Cast Members

 Posted on February 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  271 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The entire bridge crew from Star Trek: The Next Generation will return for the final season of Star Trek: Picard.

Star Trek: Picard's third season is shaping up to be a thrilling chapter in the Star Trek: Picard saga, with six cast members from Star Trek: The Next Generation returning for Picard's final season, exclusively in the U.S. on Paramount+.

LeVar Burton, Michael Dorn, Jonathan Frakes, Gates McFadden, Marina Sirtis and Brent Spiner will star alongside Stewart in Season 3.

STREAM: All-New Episodes Of Star Trek: Picard

"I remember watching the premiere of Star Trek: The Next Generation almost 34 years ago with my father like it was yesterday. It was the spark that ignited my love for science fiction," said Terry Matalas, Season 3 Showrunner and Executive Producer. "So, it's most fitting that the story of Jean-Luc Picard ends honoring the beginning, with his dearest and most loyal friends from the U.S.S. Enterprise. It would be an understatement to say that giving these characters a proper send-off is an honor. The entire Star Trek: Picard team and I can't wait for fans to experience this final, high-stakes, starship-bound adventure in Season 3!"

The announcement was made in honor of First Contact Day, the day humans and Vulcans first met, as depicted in the film Star Trek: First Contact. Since the film's debut, Star Trek fans around the world celebrate First Contact Day annually on April 5.

Stream all-new episodes of Season 2 of Star Trek: Picard on Thursdays, exclusively in the U.S. on Paramount+.

Stay tuned for more information about Season 3 of Star Trek: Picard, exclusively in the U.S. on Paramount+.
