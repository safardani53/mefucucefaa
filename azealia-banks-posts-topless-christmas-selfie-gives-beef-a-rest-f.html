 Posted on May 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  221 words  |  Martina Birk

Azealia Banks spent much of 2014 taking shots at more popular artists on social media.

T.I. threatened Banks’ life over the summer, but her parade of trash talk didn’t slow up, even for a moment.

Most recently, Banks feud with Iggy Azalea hes escalated from a good-natured beef over similar stage names to a full-blown racially-charged war of words.

The list of rappers and other artists that Azealia has cut down on Twitter is too long to reproduce here, but apparently the controversial emcee has decided to offer her rivals a Christmas truce, and give her fans the one gift everyone can enjoy – nudity!

Banks posted the above photo, along with pleas to fans to send her photos of their “bussies,” and you can just go ahead and Google that term if you’re not familiar with it, because this is a family site, gosh darn it.

Anyway, it’s nice to see her getting into the Christmas spirit. And apparently the Christmas tree. Is it just us, or does that thing look incredibly itchy?

We’re sure Ms. Bank$ will be back to throwing shade in epic fashion after the new year, (the Azealia-Eminem feud is just getting started) but for now, she’s all about peace on earth and genital-covering Christmas trees.

Oh, and bussies. The girl loves bussies.
