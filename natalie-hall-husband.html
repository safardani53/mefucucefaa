'A Very Charming Christmas Town' and 'Midnight at the Magnolia' star Natalie Hall has been turning heads, but who is her husband?

Canadian actress and singer Natalie Hall is one of our favorite stars when it comes to the TV movie genre. A mainstay of Hallmark Christmas classics, Natalie has also recently appeared in Netflix's Midnight at the Magnolia, and is set to star in the upcoming holiday film A Very Charming Christmas Town.

Who is Natalie Hall's husband?

Many viewers came to know Natalie Hall as Colby Chandler from All My Children, the soap opera she worked on from 2009 to 2011. During that time, the 30-year-old singer and actress became engaged to fellow soap star Brandon Barash, who starred on General Hospital.

It was young love, with Natalie being only 21 when Brandon popped the question. Unfortunately their relationship did not last, and they broke off their engagement before walking down the aisle.

Six years later in July 2017, Natalie married Jack H. Robbins in Vancouver's Cecil Green Park House. The gorgeous venue has been featured in many TV series and films, including Fifty Shades Freed, The Haunting of Bly Manor, Upload, and even Charmed, the reboot of which Natalie starred in for one season.

It's unclear whether work is how Natalie first became acquainted with the beautiful property, but her and Jack's day looked absolutely picture-perfect.

"The day was an absolute dream from start to finish; filled with heartfelt speeches, plenty of laughter and tears of joy, it was a day I truly didn't want to end," photographer Blush Sky writes in their Instagram page.

Natalie's husband Jack served in the U.S. Marines, according to what Natalie has posted about him on her social media pages, where she calls him her "best friend." Jack's own account is private, though he cheekily writes in his bio he is a "public figure (blue checkmark pending)."

The couple celebrated their third anniversary of marriage on July 1, which happens to also be Canada Day, an auspicious sign for the British Columbia native. "Three years ago today," she captioned a compilation of photos from their special day. "I never thought I would enjoy looking and talking to the same person for three months straight."

Natalie and Jack live in Southern California, where it appears that Jack works as a technology investment banker. After completing his time as an infantry officer stationed in Hawaii in the military, Jack earned a Masters in Business Administration at New York's Columbia Business School, and went on to work at Goldman Sachs before venturing out on his own.

If you're looking to watch Natalie in something new, there is no shortage of new projects she appears in. A Very Charming Christmas Town boasts what Natalie herself calls "fun new Christmas songs" in a "charming Danish town."

Midnight at the Magnolia, which follows "what happens when childhood friends and radio co-hosts pretend to date each other for their show," is also currently airing on Netflix.
