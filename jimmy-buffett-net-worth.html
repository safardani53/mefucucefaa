How Jimmy Buffett Accumulated His Final Net Worth

Legendary artist Jimmy Buffett passed away on Sept. 1, 2023, surrounded by friends and family at age 76. What was his net worth prior to his death?

The Gist:

  • Jimmy Buffett passed away at age 76 in September 2023.
  • He left behind an impressive legacy that didn't stop at his music and contributed to a huge net worth.
  • Jimmy's wife Jane was left in charge of his estate which is held in a private trust.
  • Jane and Jimmy shared two daughters and adopted one son who stand to inherit quite a bit.

Revered singer and shrewd businessman Jimmy Buffett died at age 76 on Sept. 1, 2023. He left behind an unforgettable legacy, with hits such as "Margaritaville" and "Cheeseburger in Paradise" to name a few.

Jimmy also left behind a long string of highly profitable business ventures such as his Margaritaville restaurants, hotels, and cruise line. Find out what else he did below and how he earned his astonishing net worth.

Jimmy Buffett's net worth was insane shortly before he died.

The singer and his legacy of albums, restaurants, and hotels are only part of the reason he left this world with an impressive net worth. Although many singers may have million-dollar net worths, Celebrity Net Worth confirms that Jimmy's final net worth clocked in at $1 billion.

Jimmy Buffett

Singer, musician, author, and businessman

Net worth: $1 billion

Jimmy Buffett released 29 studio albums between 1970 and 2020. He also created more than 30 hotels throughout the U.S., with six more hotels currently being built. However, visitors who are only looking to eat can stop by one of his many Jimmy Buffett's Margaritaville restaurants as well.

Jimmy's music, restaurants, and hotel chains of course are factors in his impressive net worth. What could also be contributing factors are the many timeshares, RV resorts, and tours he visited and participated in over the years to promote his albums and businesses.

Despite 2018 reports stating that the Margaritaville company brought in between $1.5 billion and $2 billion annually, Business Insider confirmed on April 15, 2023, that his music catalog is worth a stunning $50 million.

Jimmy Buffett's legacy will live on after his death.

Jimmy's many business and obviously his iconic music will continue to live on all around the world. Meanwhile, his widow Jane and their three kids have likely inherited a good amount of money from Jimmy. To be fair, some of the late singer's children have also been successful in the entertainment industry.

Who inherited Jimmy Buffett's money?

According to The New York Times, Jimmy's "wife of more than 40 years, Jane S. Buffett, has been tasked with carrying out his wishes after being named personal representative, or executor, of the estate." His business partner John L. Cohlan, who also serves as executive of Margaritaville Holdings, will be on hand to help Jane as needed.

Jimmy's will, which he signed in 2017, assigned his intellectual property to a trust. As far as his personal belongings go, the will suggests there is a separate list with directions regarding what should be done with these items. All of his residential real estate was left to Jimmy's wife.

The couple shares a son and two daughters though the exact nature of what they will receive isn't public due to the trust. Sarah Butters, a Florida estate lawyer, told The New York Times that, "Funneling everything to your trust affords you a ton of privacy." She then added, "There are a million celebrity wills gone wrong, but this isn’t one of them."

Jimmy Buffett shares three kids with his wife Jane.

Jimmy's oldest daughter Savannah was born in Aspen, Colo., in June 1979, per PEOPLE. Like her father, Savannah gravitated to the entertainment industry and began studying acting at a prestigious performing arts high school in South Florida. Soon the robust Los Angeles music scene brought her to Hollywood where Savannah worked in A&R. As of the time of this writing, Savannah is the host of the Savannah Daydreamin' Radio Hour on Sirius/XM.

Thirteen years after Savannah's burst onto the scene, Sarah Delaney Buffett was born in 1992. Like her sister and dad, Sarah dabbled in the entertainment field but she really shown brightly during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic. At that time, Sarah and Jimmy started a YouTube series called "Songs You Don't Know by Heart." Each episode focused on Sarah asking her dad about a lesser-known song which was followed by a performance of it. It helped Sarah and Jimmy connect with each other and his fans.

Jimmy and Jane adopted their son Cameron in 1994. Unlike his family, Cameron prefers to live a more quiet life out of the spotlight though his Instagram tells the story of a lifelong adventure-seeker. Cameron is also married and the couple appears to be incredibly devoted to a gorgeous dog.
