Molly-Mae Hague stuns in bikini selfie as torrential rain puts dampener on 10k-a-night family holi

MOLLY-MAE Hague showed off her stunning figure in a bikini selfie on her £10k-a-night holiday in the Maldives.

The influencer, 23, revealed her flat stomach in the black and white shot that saw her wearing a stylish bikini and loose linen trousers.

Resembling a Calvin Klein model, Molly wore a shirt open to complete her look in paradise.

Molly wrote: "You know it's a good holiday when it's rained every single day torrentially... and you're still having the best time."

A rare glimmer of sun threatened to appear from behind the clouds as Molly filmed from a buggy transporting her along the island's decked walkways.

She filmed a large sting ray swimming in the clear ocean and wrote: "I just don't get how they say it's completely safe to swim/get in the sea here when you constantly see huge sting rays and sharks just swimming about.

The glimpse of sun was short-lived as the heavens soon opened again and Molly filmed the downpour, writing: "The. Entire. Time. [laughing emoji]."
