Ruggero Pasquarelli Biography

He received music and acting classes as a youngster. Pasquarelli began taking singing lessons in 2008, and since 2009 he has also studied piano. He has a sibling named Leonardo Pasquarelli who is 14 years old. Pasquarelli attended the educational liceo and concentrated on Arts and Entertainment during his senior year of high school.
