90 Day The Single Life Cast, Trailer Revealed: Debbie Johnson is Back!

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  721 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Many fans are not thrilled that Big Ed Brown will be on 90 Day Fiance: Happily Ever After? Season 7.

On the flip side, at least we won’t have to see him on 90 Day: The Single Life Season 3.

That’s right! The show is of course returning, and we now know the cast. And can see the trailer!

There are returning cast members, some other newly single familiar faces, and some surprises.

On Season 2, a freshly made over Debbie Johnson dove headfirst into the world of dating. It had been a while.

Unfortunately, things did not go well for her.

In fact, a lot of her season’s drama had more to do with conflict with her son, 90 Day Fiance villain Colt Johnson.

According to the trailer, Colt will be making a comeback. And not a fun one.

Meanwhile, Debbie didn’t get laid last season. She hopes to remedy that.

We don’t know much (well, anything) about the guy licking chocolate off of her chest in the trailer, but we wish her luck.

Natalie Mordovtseva is another returning cast member.

She was not the villain in her 90 Day Fiance and Happily Ever After? seasons. She and ex Mike Youngquist shared that role.

Right now, Natalie has baby fever. She wants to start a family.

Yes, Natalie’s movements and facial expression are as over-the-top and off-putting as ever.

She goes on a date with a hot dude, but later seems to accuse him of using her for sex. (Though trailers can be misleading)

Natalie also goes to visit Mike at his home in Sequim. Obviously this wasn’t too recent, because Mike sold that property this spring.

Tania Maduro’s ex-husband, Syngin Colchester, took part in last season. Tania even appeared alongside him.

Theirs was not an especially bitter split, but they are over. It’s really for the best.

Now, Tanie is “open to meeting, men, women or any friend who will help her move on from her ex.” Good for her!

Of course, her very complicated ties to Syngin may cause problems.

In the trailer, we see a guy accuse her of lying to him because she and Syngin are still legally married.

That seems pretty goofy. It’s Kanye logic. They’re exes and they’re both moving on.

Caesar Mack is in the market for a woman who wants him for him, not for what his finances can do for her.

He’s pushing 50. He feels like the clock is ticking.

As viewers may recall, he has a particular interest in Ukrainian women.

Caesar will be enlisting the services of a professional matchmaker in Kyiv, Ukraine.

We do not know exactly when this was filmed. We have to assume that most of it was before February.

The horrors of the Russian invasion of Ukraine have bled into the lives of multiple 90 Day Fiance cast members.

Tiffany Franco has had such a toxic pattern of going on-again, off-again with Ronald Smith.

Ronald’s lack of a real job paled in comparison to his real problem — his temper.

We all watched him say things to Tiffany that should have ended their romance then and there.

Instead, they broke up and reconciled so many times that we dare not hope that they won’t reconcile this season.

We don’t know exactly when this filmed. Clearly, after her body transformation.

Many of us — including Tiffany’s detractors — would love to see her use her charm and her revenge body to find someone new.

Veronica Rodriguez has never looked for love in this franchise.

Instead, viewers know her best through her close friendship with Tim Malcolm and her commentary on Pillow Talk.

She and Tim are exes. They share a daughter. But they have both embarked on very separate romantic journeys for years.

Veronica is in the market for someone new.

However, she and Tim’s lives remain intertwined.

Based upon the trailer, that is absolutely going to come up on Season 3.

90 Day: The Single Life premieres on Monday, September 12 on Discovery Plus.

While many people view view the app with scrutiny or even disdain after Warner Bros Discovery’s recent clownery, we’ll still get the episodes months before TLC does.

It will be interesting to see how all of this plays out. Notice that the trailer didn’t include the full cast? Interesting.
