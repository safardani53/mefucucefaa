DANIELLE Colby has shown off her full belly tattoo while performing a burlesque dance to promote a new show.

The American Pickers star showed off her impressive moves to announce her newest gig in Puerto Rico.

In the clip, the longtime History Channel host wore a tiny red bikini that barely covered her.

The triangular top was completed with a flame-like design and tassels, while her bottoms featured a chain waistband and tassels that fell to the sides of her hips.

The 48-year-old had a red patterned cape tied around her neck, which she flung around to reveal her body as she showed off her burlesque moves.

BACK AND BETTER

Weeks back, Danielle shared a shocking photo on her Instagram and gave fans a deeper look into her current health problems.

The photo showed her eyes, wide open, as she held a strange animal-like creature in her mouth.

She captioned the post: "Ok, let me explain…. Today is the big day. I’ve been experiencing incredible pain through my face, jaw, ear, neck, arm and hand on my right side. A severe case of Trigeminal neuralgia is the diagnosis and a tumor in my face is the culprit."

She continued: "The last month has been excruciating but today I get my surgery to release me from this hell that visits in crashing waves of pain.

"After my surgery I will be posting my progress on my p a t r e o n. Have you ever heard of or experienced trigeminal neuralgia?"

On December 29, she assured her fans that she was doing much better in a new post.

The TV personality posed for the camera with her fiancé Jeremy Scheuch by her side.

Jeremy was pictured kissing the top of Danielle's head as they stood outdoors in warm winter ensembles.

Explained in Danielle's photo caption was everything that had come to pass for her this holiday season.

"Holidays are almost over, surgery was a success, natural pain management going well, today is my last Dr appointment and family is good, Mission accomplished!" she wrote.

"Heading back to PR very soon but we will miss our loved ones and the beautiful Midwest until our return.

"We’re excited to show end New Year’s Eve in the warm weather."
