The Summer I Turned Pretty: Who is in the cast?

 Posted on January 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  511 words  |  Larita Shotwell

THE SUMMER I Turned Pretty is based on Jenny Han's book trilogy.

The hit TV show has been renewed for a third season.

Who is in the cast of The Summer I Turned Pretty?

The Summer I Turned Pretty cast includes the following actors and actresses:

Lola Tung as Isabel "Belly" Conklin

Lola Tung, 20, plays Belly in The Summer I Turned Pretty.

The budding actress attends Carnegie Mellon University in Pennsylvania, according to Seventeen.

Jackie Chung as Laurel Conklin

Jackie Chung, 62, plays Laurel Conklin, Belly's mother.

She has also starred in Station 19 (2018) and Someone Else (2015), according to IMDb.

Rachel Blanchard as Susannah "Beck" Fisher

Rachel Blanchard, 47, took on the role of Laurel's best friend, Susannah.

In 1996, she played Cher in the Clueless TV series.

Her previous roles also include Deep Water, Degrassi, 7th Heaven, and You Me Her.

Sean Kaufman as Steven Conklin

Sean Kaufman, 23, plays Steven in The Summer I Turned Pretty.

He is known for his roles in Manifest (2018) and FBI: Most Wanted (2020), according to IMDb.

Gavin Casalegno as Jeremiah Fisher

Gavin Casalegno, 23, plays Jeremiah Fisher.

According to IMDb, the actor was also featured in I Am... Gabriel (2012), The Vampire Diaries (2015), and Walker (2021).

Christopher Briney as Conrad Fisher

Christopher Briney, 25 plays Conrad Fisher.

He has also appeared in Under the Covers (2018), Want This (2020), and Daliland (2022), according to IMDb.

Other cast members of The Summer I Turned Pretty include:

  • David Iacono as Cam
  • Alfredo Narciso as Cleveland Castillo
  • Rain Spencer as Taylor Jewel
  • Minnie Mills as Shayla
  • Summer Madison as Nicole

When did the show premiere?

The Summer I Turned Pretty debuted on June 17, 2022.

Viewers can watch the show on Amazon Prime.

In February 2021, Amazon ordered an eight-episode season of Jenny Han's book series and later that year, filming took place in North Carolina.

The coming-of-age story centers around the life and romances of a 16-year-old girl, Isabel.

On Thursday, August 3, 2023, Variety reported that the hit television series has been renewed for a third season.

According to the outlet, Amazon shared that the "greenlight was made official internally" prior to the WGA and SAG-AFTRA strikes, and that "no work on Season 3 will begin until labor negotiations between the guilds and the studios have been resolved."

Vernon Sanders, the head of television at Amazon and MGM Studios, said: "We’ve been delighted to see the passion with which fans have embraced ‘The Summer I Turned Pretty,’ making ‘Summer’ Fridays an entertainment phenomenon.
