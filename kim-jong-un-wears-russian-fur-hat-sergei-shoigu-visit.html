 Posted on February 13, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  190 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

TYRANT Kim Jong Un wears a Russian fur hat as he is greeted by war-monger Vladimir Putin’s defence minister.

The North Korean despot shook hands with Sergei Shoigu in 22C heat at Russian port city Vladivostok and they inspected bombers at an airfield.

Western forces are concerned each side may provide arms to the other.

The north Korean leader arrived in Russia last week but "will only meet Vladimir Putin's lookalike", a bizarre theory claims.

Photos showed the moment the dictator, surrounded by brief-case wielding guards, arrived in Vladivostok train station and was greeted by a Russian minister.

Russian officials welcomed the iron fist ruler as he disembarked from his luxury train, as Putin continues to attend the Eastern Economic Forum.
