MIKE Wolfe's girlfriend Leticia Cline has given fans a full tour of her newly-revamped home in Kentucky.

The model, 43, showed off the bedroom, kitchen, living room and dining room in the 7,000 square foot space.

The home features dark wood floors, exposed brick walls and a spacious open-plan layout.

Leticia showed off the navy cabinets and gold furnishings in the kitchen, as well as the dark wood dining table next to it.

The bedroom has a large bed decorated with a rustic wood headboard and long white curtains for the huge windows.

Leticia - who began dating American Pickers star Mike, 58, last year -panned around the whole of the space in two videos on her Instagram Stories on Monday.

She captioned it: "It's still a mess but after three years of construction and remodeling I'm finally almost a the end of this 7,000 sq foot building/home."

Leticia didn't specify exactly which of her properties she has been working on.

The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed in February that she owns a business empire worth nearly $200,000 in commercial property. 

She bought two suites in a two-story, 6,000 sq-ft brick building for $110,000 in her hometown of Cave City, Kentucky, in July 2021.

Leticia opened the “upscale dive atmosphere” eatery with her mom Debbie Passamore and sister Shannon Burke back in 2019.

Leticia and Mike went public with their relationship back in August of 2021.

The couple's romance began shortly after his ex-wife, Jodi Faeth, filed for divorce in November 2020.
