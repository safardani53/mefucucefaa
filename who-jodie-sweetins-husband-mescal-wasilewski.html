JODIE Sweetin is famously known for her role as Stephanie Tanner on ABC's Full House and its Netflix sequel series Fuller House.

It was announced on January 30, 2022, that Jodie Sweetin officially tied the knot with longtime boyfriend, Mescal Wasilewski.

Who is Jodie Sweetin's husband Mescal Wasilewski?

Mescal Wasilewski is known as a primary therapist at Catalyst Recovery in Los Angeles, where he has been since October 2020, according to his LinkedIn profile.

"I’ve worked in the field of addiction and mental health for over 16 years, helping to treat clients with a range of diagnosis and difficulties," his bio states.

"Much of my work has been with those suffering from addiction and various mental health issues, as well as extensive work with adolescent clients."

Prior to his career, he obtained a Bachelor's degree from California State University, Northridge as well as a master's degree in social work from Hunter College.

When did Sweetin and Wasilewski get married?

Wasilewski is famously known as Sweetin's husband.

The pair tied the knot in an intimate ceremony in Malibu on Saturday, July 30.

According to PEOPLE, Sweetin's two daughters and Full House castmates, including John Stamos and Candace Cameron Bure, were all in attendance.

"I know I have the right partner for the rest of whatever life brings me," Sweetin, 40, told the outlet. "And I couldn't be more grateful."

The pair announced their engagement in an Instagram post with Sweetin writing: "I love you Mescal, for always. You’re my person. I can’t wait to see the life that lies ahead for us.

"Here’s to us, @ghostfacelito and our life of adventures. Together."

She first confirmed their relationship in 2018, two years after her divorce from Morty Coyle was finalized.

Does Mescal Wasilewski have any children?

Wasilewski has become the stepfather to Sweetin's children Zoie and Beatrix, but at this time, it remains unclear if he has any children of his own.

Sweetin welcomed her oldest daughter in 2008 with her ex-husband Cody Herpin and then welcomed her second daughter in 2010 with her ex-husband Morty Coyle.

It is unclear if the two plan on having any children together.

What did she say about Bob Saget's death?

Bob Saget died at a Florida hotel on Sunday, January 9, 2022.

The news of Sweetin's engagement came one week after it was revealed her TV dad Saget, who portrayed the role of Danny Tanner on Full and Fuller House, had died at the age of 65.

As tributes to the late star poured in, Sweetin took to Instagram where she reflected on her relationship with Saget over the years.

"There aren’t enough words to express what I’m feeling today. Nor are they big enough to capture even a slice of who he was," she wrote.

"One thing I do know, is that we never missed a chance to tell each other, 'I love you'. Every time we talked, there were at least 3 or 4 exchanged at the end of a conversation, whether it was text, phone call or in person. And he usually had to have the last word, 'I love you more…'

"...Bob was a wonderful human being. A human being that could drive you nuts at times, and he knew it, but who was so genuine that you couldn’t even get that frustrated," she added.
