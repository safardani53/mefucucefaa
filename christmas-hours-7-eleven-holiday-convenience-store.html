Christmas hours confirmed for 7-Eleven - and it's good news if you're traveling to visit family

 Posted on March 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  338 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

SHOPPERS will be covered at all hours this holiday season - that's because 7-Eleven announced it's staying open.

Those traveling for the holidays or making any last minute stops will be pleased to hear about the store's Christmas hours.

HOLIDAY HOURS

Most 7-Eleven locations will be open 24 hours a day, including Christmas Eve and Christmas.

Customers can check the hours of their local 7-Eleven using the convenience store's locator website.

The U.S. Sun reached out to several 7-Eleven stores across the country to confirm its opening on both December 24 and 25.

All stores reached said they will be open 24 hours both days.

Read more on holiday hours

The chain is the largest of convenience stores in the US by size as of January 1, 2023.

With 13,000 US stores, 7-Eleven is regularly open 24 hours daily and has made Christmas no exception.

Regarding New Year's Eve, stores will also be open regular hours.

Be sure to check with your local 7-Eleven as holiday hours vary by location.

OTHER CONVENIENCE STORES

Several other major convenience stores and retailers have come out with their hours for the holidays.

Stores such as CVS, Duane Reade, Rite Aid, and Walgreens will be open on Christmas, but with potentially reduced hours.

Circle K is also fully open on Christmas Day.

Kohl's will also close its 1,100 stores on Christmas Day.

Walmart is another retail giant that will be closed for Christmas Day so their employees can have time off.

They will be open on Christmas Eve, but at reduced hours.

Additionally, Target stores across the US will be closed on Christmas Day.

They will, however, be open on Christmas Eve.

Home Depot has confirmed it will close all stores on Christmas Day.

Like many stores, it's also giving customers an extended time to return unwanted purchases or gifts.

Customers should call ahead to confirm these hour changes with their local stores
