MARRIED At First Sight star Ella Morgan has given fans a peek at how battered and bruised her face was following cosmetic surgery.

Ella, 29, went under the knife two years ago to have a nose job and was left with severely swollen eyes and cheeks.

She has now revealed what she looked like immediately after her operation in a collage of photos shared on social media.

One of the pictures shows Ella lying in bed and unable to even open her eyes, with her nose and cheeks covered in bandages.

Another shot shows Married At First Sight UK's first transgender bride posing in the garden once the bandages had been removed.

Her face was still extremely puffy and bruised.

Beside Ella's surgery is a snap taken before she had her invasive procedure to change her appearance.

Ella found fame in the most recent series of MAFS UK and has emerged as a breakout personality from the E4 relationship experiment.

Since the series wrapped, she has spoken about the surgery she has had over the years.

When she was 21 years old, Ella underwent gender reassignment surgery, along with multiple facial reconstruction surgeries to appear more feminine.

But she recently told Closer that she has had filler dissolved in her cheeks, chin and lips so she can "start fresh".

She told the magazine: “I know [my lips] are still quite ducky, but I love that Katie Price look. I think she looks incredible.

“I’m actually going to have my nose sorted out because I can’t breathe properly after my nose job. But the biggest thing is my forehead – I don’t like my hairline.”

Ella married Nathanial Valentino in MAFS UK, but the pair failed to hit it off and Nathanial decided to leave the show halfway through the experiment.

After Nathanial left, Ella pursued things with Bianca Petronzi's husband JJ Slater.

Their romance also didn't last and they ditched each other at the final commitment ceremony.

Ella is currently single but has described JJ as a "best friend".

She has since shared a story about JJ being diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes on Instagram, saying she is "so very proud" of him.
