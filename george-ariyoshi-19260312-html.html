George Ryoichi Ariyoshi is an American lawyer and politician who served as Hawaii's third governor from 1974 to 1986. As a Democrat, he is Hawaii's oldest governor and Asian American. first as governor of a state of America. He assumed powers and managerial functions when Governor John A. Burns was declared unfit in October 1973 and elected in 1974, becoming the first Asian American to be elected governor of any state or territory of the United States of America. His extended tenure is a record that may go uninterrupted due to term limits adopted after his departure. Ariyoshi is now considered an older politician in the Democratic Party of Hawaii.
