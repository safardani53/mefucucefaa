REALITY TV show Southern Charm made well-recognized stars out of several of its cast members.

During the Bravo program's 7th season, Taylor Ann Green was introduced as Shep Rose's girlfriend.

Who is Southern Charm star Taylor Ann Green?

Born on October 14, 1994, Taylor Ann Green is an American reality TV star from Asheville, North Carolina.

In 2015, she graduated with her bachelor's in business administration, hospitality, and tourism management from Appalachian State University.

Prior to her Southern Charm fame, Taylor worked as a clinical assistant at Kelley & Gardner Orthodontics.

She has since moved on to an account executive position at Associate Staffing.

When Southern Charm's 7th season premiered in October 2020, Taylor was introduced to viewers as co-star Shep Rose's significant other.

By the time season 8 debuted, she was promoted to a series regular.

How long was Taylor with Shep Rose?

Taylor and Shep Rose dated for two years; they first sparked relationship rumors in March 2020.

For months leading up to Southern Charm's 7th season, sleuths noticed Taylor and Shep posting affectionate pictures on Instagram.

By May of the same year, the pair confirmed they were together.

At the time, Shep told Us Weekly about his romance with Taylor: "Things are going well and she’s fantastic.

"I kinda jokingly said, like, she refuses to give me a reason to not be in love with her, honestly.

"Day after day, month after month, she was just fantastic.

"It just makes it really easy when, when you’re, like...'Wait a second, why would I ever not want this person in my life?'

"That doesn’t make any sense at all. So it’s definitely a departure from my norm."

On July 20, 2022, it was confirmed Taylor and Shep split.

"We spent two and a half years together. You’ve allegedly slept with over 300 women," Taylor said about their break up at the program's 8th season reunion special.

When asked for her side of the story, she directed her comments at her ex-boyfriend and shared: "You went out. You partied. Now, you’ve wrapped me into this slew of whores who you’ve been f**king.

"Now, I’m just a number, and that’s what f**ked me up in the head. I was just the whore that lasted the longest."

Sometime after the season 8 reunion, Shep explained to the above-mentioned outlet: "There were some [issues] in the way that we looked at the world.

"[I will] always be in love with her. I love her. That’s all you need to know.

"She’s fantastic [but to] get back together, I don’t know. I think that maybe I’m not the best for her. It sounds like a cop-out. I swear it’s not."

What happened to Taylor's brother Worth Green?

On June 12, 2023, it was revealed Taylor's older brother Richard Worthington Green died at the age of 36.

His death occurred on June 8; the announcement was made by Taylor and Richard's sister, Catie.

"Worth left this world on top of the world - he had a beautiful passion for life and cared deeply for those around him," the family told PEOPLE.
