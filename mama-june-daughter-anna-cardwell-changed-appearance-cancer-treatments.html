FAMILY SUPPORT

Anna's family has been rallying around her during her battle.

She has three sisters - Alana Thompson, known as Honey Boo Boo; Lauryn Efird, known as Pumpkin; and Jessica Shannon, known as Chubbs.

Anna and her mom, June, have had a complicated relationship for many years - but Anna did attend June's wedding when she got married to Justin Stroud in January.

Anna previously told The U.S. Sun that she has "no relationship" with June, claiming she's "never apologized" for her past mistakes.

Her bond with the TLC star broke after her mother rekindled a relationship with the man accused of molesting her as a child.

Speaking to The U.S. Sun in June 2021, Anna said they never had a conversation about the incident.

“You know, I've never had a conversation with her where she was like ‘Oh I'm sorry for what happened or, I'm sorry for this.

"It’s like she is always brushing everything off and like acting like everything is fine and nothing happened.” 

FAMILY FEUD

However, earlier this week, June revealed a major feud with one family member - and accused her of "not showing up" for Anna's cancer treatments.

In an exclusive interview with The U.S. Sun, June alleged that her sister, Joanne Shannon, known as Doe Doe, has not been involved at all, despite their previously close relationship.

When asked about her relationship with her sister, June said: "We don't talk to her, and she hasn't been around since Anna found out she has cancer.

"She knows every time she goes to the doctor, she has not been around the family since Anna has had cancer.

"She hasn't shown up for one treatment. She has not shown up at all," June lashed out.

"Sometimes people do stuff for show, not just wherever.

"Some people don't do stuff behind closed doors, I always say do stuff when the cameras aren't watching. Do stuff when the cameras ain't on."

June said she told Anna the following regarding family members who have reportedly not offered support: "I tell her everybody knows what's going on, and they have to live at the end of the day with their own decisions when that time comes if she passes."

Doe Doe has not yet responded to a request for comment.
