Holly Willoughby squeals as Phillip Schofield almost throws up when he eats vomit-flavoured ad

 Posted on October 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  300 words  |  Aldo Pusey

HOLLY Willoughby squealed as Phillip Schofield almost threw up when he ate a "vomit-flavoured" sweet.

Jelly Belly Bean's £15 Boozled Advent Calendar gives a mix of nice - and disgusting - flavour of sweets.

Explaining what it is on today's This Morning, Holly said: "You say the odd one could be horrible... but there's spoiled milk, dead fish flavour, skunk spray, mouldy cheese, or bogie-flavoured.

"Oh my God there's a vomit-flavoured one too. What is wrong with them?"

At first Phil got lucky when he got a chocolate jelly bean - but it all went downhill when he went for another.

He suddenly started shouting: "I've got vomit, I've got vomit!"

Phil then asked Holly, "hold my hair back".

The presenter looked like he was about to gag as he raised his hand to his mouth.

Holly had warned him not to try it, as she explained she would not be giving it a go on air.

Instead she watched Phil get caught out by the horrid taste.

Despite advent calendars being a Christmas tradition, Holly was not willing to back down on having the Boozled brand in her home.

She's been getting in the festive spirit lately with a trip to Winter Wonderland with her kids.

Holly shared a rare snap of her brood at Winter Wonderland during a "dreamy" family night out.

But fans were quick to question whether the Christmas attraction had been completely shut down for the ITV star.

"It looks a bit quiet?!" another wrote.

"No crowds?!! You lucky thing!!!" a third added.
