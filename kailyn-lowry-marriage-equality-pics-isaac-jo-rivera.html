Kail & Javi Pose In Pictures Supporting Marriage Equality

The series of shots feature Kailyn, Javi, Isaac and two friends. In one picture, Javi and a male friend hold Isaac, each kissing one of his cheeks. Kailyn and a female friend gaze lovingly into each other’s eyes in another, both wearing white wedding gowns.

In the final pic, the women and baby Isaac stand on one side and the men on the other, separated by a banner that reads “all love & no hate.”

Jo Tweets That Pics Make Him ‘Sick’

While the photos may be for a good cause, not everyone is a fan of them. Jo took to Twitter to express his disgust over the images, responding to a fan who posted the picture of Isaac in between the two men and asked, “Doesn’t this make you sick? You shouldn’t allow this. It’s just not right.”

Jo responded, “yes it does… Even Isaac is like wtf is going on here…”

Another Twitter follower tweeted Jo, asking if he was in favor of equality. Jo responded, “…I’m all for equality. But I don’t see how that photo has anything to do with representing equality.”

Kailyn & Isaac: A History of NOH8

While the release of Kailyn’s most recent marriage equality pics may have angered Jo, this wasn’t the first time Kail has involved her young son in the movement. The two posed for the NOH8 Campaign in Nov. 2011.
