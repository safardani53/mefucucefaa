Teanna and Jayda’s Twitter feud happened not even a full two weeks after Ms. London, who is also an adult film star, hinted that Lil Baby allegedly paid her $6,000 to have sex. “Ok I’m home, 6K richer & full of 1942,” she tweeted, followed by, “The best d*** I ever had came from a millionaire who didn’t say more than two full sentences to me.” Up until that point, Ms. London had not mentioned anyone. Lil Baby and Jayda have been in an off-again, on-again relationship since about 2016. While Jayda tweeted that they’re “no longer in a relationship” in March of 2020, she and Lil Baby have since reunited. The couple share a son together, Loyal, whom they welcomed in Feb. 2019. Jayda has also appeared in Lil Baby’s music videos for “Catch the Sun” and “Close Friends.”
