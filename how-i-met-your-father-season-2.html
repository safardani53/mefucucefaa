Hilary Duff Announces 'How I Met Your Father' Season 2 Premiere Date

 Posted on March 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  434 words  |  Larita Shotwell

'How I Met Your Father' Season 2: Actress Hilary Duff took to Instagram to share the official premiere date for the hit Hulu spinoff series.

Kids, this is the tale of how Sophie's (Hilary Duff) love story got renewed for a second season.

How I Met Your Father is a Hulu spinoff series of How I Met Your Mother. This time around it's an older Sophie (Kim Cattrall) telling her son the story of how she met his father.

Younger Sophie is a 30-year-old New Yorker doing her best to find love in the city that never sleeps. Older Sophie teased to her son that she met his father the night of Sid's (Suraj Sharma) engagement party. So far, that means the candidates are Jesse (Chris Lowell), Drew (Josh Peck), or Ian (Daniel Augustin). Could there be another father candidate in Sophie's future? Do we have another season to look forward to in order to find out?

When will Season 2 of 'How I Met Your Father' premiere on Hulu?

Good news, How I Met Your Father fans! Hulu has officially announced that the show has been renewed for a second season. Not only that but Season 2 will have 20 episodes in total. That's definitely a season episode upgrade from the first season's 10-episode count.

Hulu officially announced that Season 2 will premiere on Tuesday, Jan. 24, with new episodes airing weekly on the streaming platform.

'How I Met Your Father' Season 2: Hilary Duff has been sharing pics from the set.

Given Hilary's dedication to delivering quality How I Met Your Father content on social media, fans speculated that the series would return in early 2023. On Feb. 15, 2022, she captioned a pic of the How I Met Your Father cast, writing, "HAPPY NEWS! Surprise! You'll be seeing more great chapters in this love story because #HIMYF has been renewed for Season 2!"

Since then, the former Disney Channel actress has been super on point with giving fans behind-the-scenes content when she was on-set filming How I Met Your Father.

In October, Hilary shared a picture of herself alongside an identically dressed mannequin, which she captioned: "A spitting image. Also a glamorous peek inside our wardrobe room."

Most recently, Hilary confirmed the Season 2 premiere news on social media with a video of herself announcing the official 2023 airdate.

"The gang is back!" she wrote, adding, "Season 2 of #HIMYF premieres on @hulu January 24!"

Well, we certainly can't wait to tune in.
