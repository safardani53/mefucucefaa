Remember when there was talk of a forthcoming Drake and Rihanna wedding?

Remember when Drake made all warm-blooded females melt with the way he talked about Rihanna at the MTV Video Music Awards at the end of August?

That was mere weeks ago, but a new report suggests the relationship between these singers is already over.

According to Life & Style, Rihanna has broken up with Drake because the artist has allegedly grown way too close to an Instagram model named Dakota Gonzalez.

Has Drake actually done the deed with her? Have the two danced he horizontal mambo?

The report is unclear, as an anonymous source simply tells the tabloid that Rihanna "was furious after [Drake] stayed by [Gonzalez’s] side and showed her off at an afterparty last month in Vegas."

Why would Drake possibly cheat on his self-described dream girl?
