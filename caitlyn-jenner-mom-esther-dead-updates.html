Interesting facts about Caitlyn Jenner, continued

Despite having two failed marriages before Kris Jenner, Caitlyn married the mother of Kim Kardashian after only five months of dating in 1991 and they share two daughters Kendall and Kylie Jenner.

After graduating from university, Caitlyn married her first wife Chrystie Crownover.

During their marriage, the pair had son Burt and daughter Casey but divorced in 1980 after nine years of marriage.

On January 5, 1981, Caitlyn married actress Linda Thompson in Hawaii and the couple had two sons together, Brandon and Brody Jenner, but by 1986 they had separated.

Interesting facts about Caitlyn Jenner

Caitlyn Jenner, formerly known as Bruce Jenner, won a football scholarship to Graceland College in Iowa.

However, she had to quit the game after a knee injury, at which point the Graceland track coach then encouraged Caitlyn to try her hand at the decathlon.

Caitlyn later went on to win the gold medal for the event at the Montreal Olympic Games in 1976, earning the unofficial title of the “World’s Greatest Athlete.”

Meet Caitlyn Jenner’s father: William Jenner

Caitlyn’s father is William Jenner.

It is understood he was a tree surgeon before he retired.

He has appeared alongside Caitlyn at several events in the past, but he remained much more out of the spotlight than Caitlyn’s mother Esther.

William died on December 28, 2000.

All about Esther Jenner, conclusion

In an interview, Esther described the show as “plotless,” adding: “I wasn’t fond of that program. There was no plot, and why it was such a hit I’ll never know.”

“When your child becomes, oh golly, world-famous, it’s very difficult for a mother to put them in that category, she’s not a celebrity to me, she’s my kid.”

She added: “It’s just very difficult, but I’m no different than any other mother, I love my kids.”

Esther also branded Caitlyn’s appearance on I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! “torture” to watch, vowing not to tune in to the show.

In an exclusive interview with Sun Online, Esther blasted: “That’s not entertainment to me, to see somebody frightened. I think she could spend a lot more pleasant times on the golf course.”

All about Esther Jenner, continued

Esther was rather critical of Caitlyn’s ex-wife, and mother to Kendall and Kylie.

She alleged Kris was hyper-controlling and an unsuitable choice of spouse, although she has since said their relationship has improved.

Esther also criticized the hugely popular Keeping Up With The Kardashians, which features Caitlyn’s family.

All about Esther Jenner

Caitlyn Jenner was born Bruce Jenner on October 28, 1949.

Her parents Esther and William raised her just outside of New York City.

Caitlyn was known as Bruce until June 2015, when she announced she was transitioning, and then had gender reassignment surgery in January 2017.

Caitlyn had a rather different upbringing from her mother, who grew up on a small farm where her family had three cows, a flock of chickens, and grew their own vegetables.

But the pair were understood to be pretty close, so much so that they speak on the phone almost every day.

And Esther, in her 90s, was very supportive of Caitlyn’s transition, saying: “It’s nothing she asked for, it’s a condition and it starts in the mother’s womb.”
