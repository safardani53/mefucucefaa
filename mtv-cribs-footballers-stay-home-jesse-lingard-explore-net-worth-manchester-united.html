MTV Cribs is back for a brand new series featuring some of the world’s biggest football players. Episode 1 sees Jesse Lingard welcome viewers into his home but just what do we know about the Manchester United star?

Thanks to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, we’ve all been spending much more time at home than we’re used to.

For many, it’s left them on the verge of madness after spending months being cooped up inside the same four walls.

To help with that, MTV’s Cribs is returning to our screens for a brand new series that will give us a sneak peek into the homes of some of the world’s biggest football stars.

Episode 1 of the new series arrives on June 1st and takes us inside the home of Manchester United star, Jesse Lingard

MTV Cribs: Footballers Stay Home

MTV Cribs has been around since the year 2000 but now, in 2020, the first-ever series to be based purely on football players has arrived.

Starting on June 1st, MTV Cribs: Footballers Stay Home will take us inside the multi-million-pound homes of football stars from around the world.

Episode 1, which is due to air at 8:30pm on MTV, will introduce viewers to Jesse Lingard and his magnificent Manchester mansion.

MTV

Who is Jesse Lingard?

Jesse Lingard, who is 27 years old, plays for Premier League giants, Manchester United.

Born in Warrington, Lingard has risen through the ranks of United’s academy and is now a regular in the first team and has won several major trophies with the team.

On top of that, the midfielder is also a member of the England team and, as we see in the MTV Cribs episode, he currently has 28 caps for the national team which, bizarrely, he keeps in the same cabinet as his underwear.

MTV

What’s Jesse Lingard’s salary and net worth?

According to Goal, Jesse Lingard’s net worth is estimated at between £6 and £11 million.

In terms of his salary, during the 2016/17 season, Lingard signed a new contract with Manchester United that was worth a reported £100,000 per week.

Over the course of a full year, that would equate to a cool £5.2 million and as the contract was due to run for four years, is worth just under £21 million in total and will no doubt keep Lingard’s net worth on the rise.

MTV Cribs: Footballers Stay Home, featuring Jesse Lingard in episode 1, is due to start on June 1st at 8:30pm on MTV.
