SINGER Elle King is most famously known for her 2015 song, Ex’s And Oh’s.

Away from the musical spotlight, she can be found spending time with her fiancé, Dan Tooker, and their child.

Who is Elle King's fiancé Dan Tooker?

In 2016, King married Andrew Ferguson, who she met in a hotel lobby.

After their chance encounter, the former couple got engaged 12 days later on a sailing trip around San Francisco Bay.

However, the pair divorced in 2017 in what she called a “destructive marriage,” telling PEOPLE that she “is stronger now because of it."

In 2018, King then got engaged to tattoo practitioner, Dan Tooker, who is also the father of her first child.

Who is Elle King?

Elle King was born in Los Angeles, California, on July 3, 1989.

The 33-year-old's real name is Tanner Elle Schneider, but she is more famously known as an American singer, songwriter, and actress.

King made her Hollywood back in 1999 in her father's film, Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo.

In 2012, she released her debut EP, The Elle King EP, with the label RCA.

Her track, Playing For Keeps, became the theme song for VH1's Mob Wives Chicago series.

Three years later, King released her debut album, Love Stuff, and earned two Grammy Award nominations.

Her other two albums include Shake The Spirit (2018) and Come Get Your Wife (2023).

King has toured with artists such as Of Monsters And Men, Train, James Bay, The Chicks, Heart, and Miranda Lambert.

What is Elle King’s net worth?

King has an estimated net worth of $4million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

She has reportedly amassed her fortune through the release of her albums and singles.

Who is Elle King's father?

King is the daughter of Rob Schneider and London King.

Schneider, 57, is known as an American actor, comedian, screenwriter and director, while King's mom is known as a former model and actress.

In 2021, the Saturday Night Live alum made headlines after he was revealed as the Hamster on season 6 of The Masked Singer.

Over the years, King and Schneider have had a rocky relationship after he remarried and started a new family, but they have since rekindled and he is known as her No.1 fan.

"We both have grown up a lot. And you realize what’s important," King told PEOPLE back in 2018.
