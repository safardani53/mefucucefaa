 Posted on March 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  535 words  |  Martina Birk

A GLAMOROUS woman in her 50s has stunned in a selection of Grace Karin dresses to wear during the party season.

The five-foot-tall fashionista shared her shopping haul after struggling to decide which dress to keep in her wardrobe until New Year’s Eve. 

The style enthusiast who posts under the handle @justnina_official boasts almost 139,000 followers on TikTok, where she routinely shares product reviews.

She took to the social media platform to share a selection of Grace Karin dresses that are available up to a size 22.

Red halterneck dress

The trendsetter started the haul wearing a sparkly halterneck red dress which cost just over $34. 

The sequin bodycon dress is also sold in gold, rose gold, silver, and black.

She styled her hair in a top knot with drop earrings and metallic-heeled sandals.

“Which do you like best, one to five?” read the text over the video.

Black wrap dress 

She kept the sequined party dress theme going as she put on a black wrap-style dress. 

The TikToker stunned in the $42 dress while still wearing the same heels and hairstyle.

Shoppers on the social media app have given it a 4.2-star rating and penned glowing reviews.

“I was expecting a cheap version of this dress but got a really well-made, good cut, and weighty dress! I would recommend it,” one customer said. 

Tie waist one-shoulder dress

Nina looked chic in a one-shoulder sparkly green dress with a thigh-high split. 

“Slip-proof band,” read the text over the video as she showed how the dress stays securely in place on her chest.

She posed from different angles in the belted dress which is also sold in deep pink, black, and purple for $40.

Floral print ruffle dress 

The fashionista shook her hips from side to side as she wowed in a ruffle detail bodycon dress. 

She revealed the dress can also be worn with the straps tucked in for the illusion of a strapless frock. 

The $40 dress has a 4.9-star rating on the app and is available in two different colors.

“The most comfortable evening dress that I’ve ever purchased. It was flattering and I got so many compliments,” one reviewer wrote.

“Absolutely stunning dress!! Hugs my curves so well, makes me feel great in it!! Stretchy and soft material, can’t wait to wear it out!” another shopper said.

Belted green midi dress 

She finished the haul by trying on an elegant off-shoulder bodycon dress also embellished with sequins.

The $39 asymmetric dress has a 4.8-star rating from happy shoppers. 

“5ft and wearing a small. They’re all stretchy and fully lined,” she captioned the post.

“Amazing quality for the price! Which do you like best?”

Thousands of people liked the video and took to the comment section to share their opinions.

The majority of viewers said they were wowed by the red dress that started the try-on haul.

“They all look stunning on you, but the red one is my favorite,” one person wrote.

“Red is beautiful on you,” a second commented.
