Get to know BTS member Jungkook

Jung Kook also known as Jungkook is a South Korean singer-songwriter.

He is the main vocalist of the best-selling band in South Korean history, BTS.

In 2013, the young Korean pop singer made his debut as a member of BTS with the release of the single 2 Cool 4 Skool.

In 2016, the group released their second Korean studio album, Wings – which became the first album to sell one million copies in South Korea.

By 2017, BTS crossed into the global music market, leading the Korean wave into the United States and breaking sales records.

We owe it all to RM

Jungkook chose Big Hit Entertainment over JYP Entertainment, FNC Entertainment, TS Entertainment, and other entertainment businesses.

Jungkook has stated on various occasions that he did not know many musicians when he lived in Busan.

But he was taken by RM's rapping abilities and wanted to learn more about his firm.

When he competed in Superstar K, he met Big Hit's owner Bang Si-hyuk and discovered that RM was a trainee there.

Jungkook, aged 15, made the decision to work with his idol and joined the Seoul-based label.

BTS hiatus misconception

After a few very successful years, BTS will be taking some time to focus on solo projects.

“BTS are not taking a hiatus. Members will be focusing more on solo projects at this time,” stated Hybe back in June.

In June, fans were shocked to hear this news. Some thought the group was taking a break or disbanding, but that is not the case.

“We have to accept that we’ve changed,” shared RM.

“For me, it was like the group BTS was within my grasp until ‘On’ and ‘Dynamite,’ but after ‘Butter’ and ‘Permission to Dance,’ I didn’t know what kind of group we were anymore,” shared BTS leader RM.

With their solo projects, the members assured the fans that they will be a better version of themselves, per USA Today.
